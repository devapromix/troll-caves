Rangers are incredibly destructive in combat and strike terror and fear
into their enemies with their immense strength and incredible speed.
Thanks to a large number of skills that attack a single target, they
are able to trackdown the victim and kill them at the right moment.
Enemies will meet their death by dying from a huge number of wounds
and bleeding, causing damage over time.

Weapon: hunter bow
Armor:  quilted armor

Master's haversack, light quiver, craft box
